Teambuilding

Overlook Resort has been hosting and organizing teambuilding activities and events for companies large and small in the last 19 years. Overlook sits on a hillside in Antipolo overlooking the city. The resort is located in a wooded area, near historic and famous Hinulugang Taktak Waterfalls and Park, surrounded by hills covered with trees. It is walking distance from the Taktak Falls and park where we sometimes hold picnics for our company guests. The resort has a large open area for holding games and sports activities.The parking lot can hold up to 30 cars. Overlook is a corporate member of Eastridge Golf (20 minutes from the resort) where guests can play golf. It is near Ynares sports park where guests can play softball and other games.

Benefits of Teambuilding

It is a proven fact that happy workers are more efficient and have a lower rate of error than those who are unhappy in their position. Use this concept to keep employees interested in the information you have to share, get creative, and build the energy your business needs to succeed. Many companies choose to provide an annual company outing to build morale, show appreciation, emphasize your company's commitment to the employees, encourage employee interaction, strengthen teamwork, and reduce stress.
